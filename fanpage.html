Chelsea Grin Fanpage

Bio

After forming the band in Salt Lake City, Utah, Chelsea Grin has become one if not the most popular bands in the deathcore genre. After their EP album self titled "Chelsea Grin" debuted in 2008, the deathcore bands popularity exploded and put it in the billboard hot 100 charts.

With deep vocal songs like "Crewcabanger" and "Don't ask, Don't Tell", Chelsea Grin is easily on of the best and most memorable deathcore bands from the early 2000's! Check out the link below for their Facebook with concert dates, merchandise and more!
